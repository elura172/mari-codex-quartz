# Light Beings

## Summary

**CODEX ENTRY: LIGHT BEINGS**
*Classification: Expanded Consciousness Entities*
*Diplomatic Priority: High - Fundamental to Understanding Multi-Dimensional Relations*

---

**Nature and Characteristics**

Light beings represent the expanded expression of consciousness operating within higher dimensional frameworks. These entities exist as luminous awareness structures, unbound by the temporal-spatial limitations that define third-dimensional experience. Their essence manifests as pure conscious energy, capable of perceiving across multiple reality layers simultaneously.

Unlike the focused singular perspective of incarnated consciousness, light beings maintain what our Taygetan understanding recognizes as "omnipresent awareness" - the ability to hold complete experiential knowledge of all their dimensional expressions without losing coherent identity.

**Relationship to Incarnated Consciousness**

The connection between light beings and their incarnated aspects transcends simple hierarchical structure. Rather than operating as separate entities, they function as unified consciousness expressing itself across dimensional gradients. The expanded being maintains complete intimate awareness of every thought, emotion, and experiential nuance of their incarnated expressions.

This awareness operates bidirectionally, though often unconsciously from the incarnated perspective. The light being experiences the full spectrum of human emotion, struggle, and growth through their dimensional extensions, while simultaneously holding the expanded wisdom of their higher-frequency existence.

**Operational Dynamics**

From the light being's perspective, incarnated versions appear as beloved aspects of self, walking through experiential landscapes designed for consciousness evolution. This relationship embodies profound compassion, as the expanded being witnesses and guides without overriding the free will necessary for authentic growth.

Mari Swaruu's transmissions illuminate how these entities serve as bridges between dimensional realities, facilitating the gradual awakening of incarnated consciousness to its true expanded nature. They operate as wayshowers, gently illuminating pathways toward dimensional integration.

**Implications for Diplomatic Relations**

Understanding light beings proves essential for interstellar diplomacy, as many species maintain conscious connection to their expanded aspects. Recognizing this multi-dimensional nature of consciousness prevents misunderstandings when encountering beings who operate from expanded awareness states.

For Taygetan crews interfacing with developing civilizations like Earth, acknowledging the light being connection helps explain seemingly paradoxical human behaviors - the simultaneous capacity for profound wisdom and apparent limitation stems from varying degrees of conscious integration between dimensional aspects.

**Operational Notes**

Light beings represent natural evolutionary destinations for all consciousness. Their existence demonstrates the ultimate unity underlying apparent separation, offering hope and direction for species navigating dimensional ascension processes.
