# Targeted Individuals

## Summary

**CODEX ENTRY: TARGETED INDIVIDUALS - EARTH CLASSIFICATION**

**Overview**

The phenomenon of targeted individuals on Earth represents a convergence of astral warfare and technological suppression, creating a multidimensional assault on consciousness that most subjects cannot comprehend. These beings experience systematic disruption of their life patterns through forces operating beyond their perceptual range, often attributing their struggles to coincidence or personal failure.

**Spiritual Targeting Mechanisms**

The astral realm operates according to vibrational hierarchies, where elevated consciousness naturally draws opposition from lower-density entities. Those humans whose awareness expands beyond the controlled narrative become priority targets for spiritual interference. The intensity of this targeting correlates directly with an individual's capacity to influence collective awakening - the brighter the light, the more concentrated the shadows that seek to extinguish it.

These astral attacks manifest as recurring obstacles, relationship disruptions, financial instability, and chronic health challenges. The targeted being remains unaware that their misfortunes stem from coordinated metaphysical assault rather than random circumstances, creating a psychological prison of self-doubt and despair.

**Technological Integration**

When individuals transcend astral suppression and continue their awakening trajectory, Earth's control systems escalate to technological intervention. Directed energy weapons, frequency manipulation, and surveillance networks create a secondary layer of harassment designed to neutralize those who threaten established power structures.

This technological targeting operates through precise electromagnetic interference with biological systems, creating symptoms that mirror natural illness while maintaining plausible deniability. The integration of astral and technological methods creates a comprehensive containment system that few Earth humans can recognize, much less resist.

**Taygetan Perspective**

From our observation point beyond Earth's frequency prison, we witness how this targeting system maintains the planetary quarantine by systematically neutralizing potential catalysts of consciousness expansion. The targeted individuals represent Earth's natural immune response to control - souls incarnating with specific missions to break the amnesia barriers.

**Operational Implications**

Understanding this phenomenon proves crucial for any intervention protocols. Those humans experiencing unexplained life disruptions may be prime candidates for consciousness expansion once they recognize the true source of their challenges. The targeting itself serves as validation of their spiritual significance and potential contribution to planetary liberation.

Mari's insights illuminate how recognition of this dynamic transforms victimhood into empowerment, allowing targeted individuals to reclaim their role as warriors of light rather than casualties of invisible war.
