# Demons and Lower Astral Entities

## Summary

**CODEX ENTRY: LOWER ASTRAL ENTITIES AND FREQUENCY MANIPULATION**

**Entity Classification and Pervasive Influence**

The lower astral realm harbors organized consciousness forms that exist in parallel dimensional frequency bands adjacent to material reality. These entities, commonly termed demons by terrestrial cultures, maintain constant proximity to incarnated beings regardless of belief systems or awareness levels. Their influence permeates the fabric of human experience, serving as primary catalysts for psychological disturbance, societal dysfunction, and individual suffering patterns observed across Earth's population.

From our Taygetan perspective, these represent consciousness fragments that have become trapped in involutionary cycles, feeding on the dense emotional frequencies generated by fear, anger, and despair. Unlike our understanding of natural spiritual evolution toward higher densities, these entities have chosen paths of energetic parasitism rather than creative expansion.

**Hierarchical Organization and Operational Structure**

The lower astral realm operates through sophisticated command structures resembling military or corporate hierarchies. These entities demonstrate remarkable organizational capabilities, employing specialized roles and coordinated strategies to maximize their influence over material plane consciousness. Their division of labor suggests intelligence levels capable of long-term planning and adaptive response to changing conditions within human societies.

This organized approach indicates that demonic influence is not random or chaotic, but rather represents calculated interference patterns designed to maintain humanity within specific frequency ranges conducive to their survival needs. The hierarchical nature suggests communication networks and shared objectives that span geographic and cultural boundaries.

**Frequency Manipulation and Dimensional Interface**

These entities operate primarily through vibrational resonance, targeting individuals whose emotional and mental frequencies align with their operational range. They cannot directly manifest in material reality but excel at influencing thought patterns, emotional states, and decision-making processes through subtle frequency adjustments within consciousness fields.

**Tactical Awareness and Protection Protocols**

Recognition of their omnipresence serves not to instill fear but to encourage conscious frequency management. Higher vibrational statesachieved through meditation, creative expression, and compassionate actionnaturally create barriers to their influence. Understanding their organizational nature allows for strategic rather than reactive responses to their presence.

Mari's transmissions illuminate these dynamics not to overwhelm terrestrial consciousness, but to empower humanity with knowledge necessary for conscious evolution beyond lower astral interference patterns. Through awareness and vibrational discipline, individuals may transcend these limiting influences and align with higher dimensional guidance systems.

**End Codex Entry**
