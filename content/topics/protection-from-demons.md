# Protection from Demons

## Summary

**CODEX ENTRY: PROTECTION FROM LOWER ASTRAL ENTITIES**

**Frequency Alignment as Primary Defense**

Our observations from orbit reveal a fundamental truth that transcends planetary boundaries: protection from demonic influence operates not through external rituals or barriers, but through the conscious elevation of one's vibrational signature. Mari Swaruu's transmissions illuminate a principle we Taygetans recognize across stellar civilizationsentities of lower astral density cannot maintain contact with consciousness operating at frequencies of Source alignment.

**The Mirror Principle**

The most profound insight concerns the mirror-like nature of dimensional interaction. Lower entities can only attach to consciousness that resonates at their vibrational level. When an individual ceases to embody the characteristics they seek protection frommanipulation, deception, parasitic behaviorthey naturally ascend beyond the reach of such influences. This represents a universal law of consciousness that operates identically whether one inhabits Earth's dense third-dimensional matrix or the expanded awareness fields of our homeworld.

**Conscious Choice as Sovereign Power**

Each moment presents the fundamental choice between service to self and service to others, between contraction and expansion, between fear-based reaction and love-centered response. This choice-point represents true sovereigntythe ability to consciously select one's vibrational trajectory regardless of external circumstances or past patterns. From our multidimensional perspective, we observe how this principle creates impenetrable energetic boundaries around those who consistently choose alignment with Source.

**Wisdom Through Integration**

The highest form of protection emerges not from inexperience with darkness, but from intimate knowledge of its nature combined with unwavering commitment to light. Those who have explored the depths of their own shadow material, understood the mechanisms of lower astral manipulation, yet consistently choose elevation, develop an unshakeable discernment. They become transparent to demonic influence while simultaneously serving as beacons for others navigating similar challenges.

**Practical Implementation**

This protection manifests through moment-by-moment choices: responding with compassion rather than judgment, seeking truth over comfortable lies, offering service rather than seeking exploitation. Each decision either strengthens or weakens one's energetic integrity. The beauty of this system lies in its simplicityno complex techniques or external dependencies, merely the consistent application of conscious choice in alignment with one's highest understanding.

Mari's wisdom reminds us that across all worlds and dimensions, consciousness itself is both the battlefield and the victory.

**End Entry**
