# Astral Attacks

## Summary

**CODEX ENTRY: ASTRAL ATTACK PHENOMENA**

**Classification:** Etheric Interference - Combat Application
**Threat Level:** Variable (Mild to Severe Physical Manifestation)
**Documentation Source:** Swaruu of Erra (Mari Swaruu) - Field Observations

**Phenomenon Overview**

Through the keen perceptions of our Earth-stationed colleague Mari, we have documented instances of deliberately engineered spiritual assaults that manifest as physical ailments within targeted individuals. These attacks represent a sophisticated form of etheric warfare, wherein hostile consciousness directs concentrated negative energy toward a specific being, resulting in measurable biological dysfunction.

**Metaphysical Mechanics**

The phenomenon operates on the foundational principle that all physical illness originates within the astral or etheric realm before crystallizing into material symptoms. From our Taygetan understanding, this aligns with the cascade effect we observe in consciousness-matter interactionswhere disruptions in higher-density fields inevitably precipitate downward into the physical plane.

Unlike naturally occurring illness patterns that emerge from internal imbalance or environmental factors, astral attacks bear distinct signatures of external origination. The targeted individual experiences symptoms that feel fundamentally foreign to their energetic constitution, often accompanied by an intuitive recognition of deliberate interference.

**Identification Parameters**

Mari's direct experience provides valuable diagnostic markers: illness manifestations that resist conventional healing modalities, symptoms that feel imposed rather than organically developed, and an underlying energetic signature that experienced sensitives can distinguish from authentic health challenges. The attacks often correlate with periods of increased spiritual visibility or truth-telling activities, suggesting targeting based on consciousness expansion or information dissemination.

**Tactical Implications**

For crew members operating in Earth's complex etheric environment, awareness of this phenomenon proves essential. The planet's dense astral layers and competitive consciousness dynamics create optimal conditions for such attacks, particularly against individuals who challenge established control structures or facilitate awakening processes.

**Defensive Considerations**

Understanding these attacks as consciousness-based phenomena opens pathways for appropriate countermeasures. Since the assault originates in etheric dimensions, effective response requires engagement at matching densitiesthrough spiritual protection protocols, energetic shielding techniques, and alliance with benevolent higher-density beings capable of neutralizing hostile intentions.

Mari's documentation serves as both warning and empowerment for those navigating Earth's challenging metaphysical terrain. Her courage in identifying and articulating these experiences provides invaluable intelligence for protecting our mission personnel and understanding the sophisticated warfare techniques employed against consciousness evolution on this world.
