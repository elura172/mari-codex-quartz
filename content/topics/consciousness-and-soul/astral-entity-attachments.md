# Astral Entity Attachments

## Summary

**CODEX ENTRY: ASTRAL ENTITY ATTACHMENTS**
*Classification: Metaphysical Phenomena - Parasitic Interface*
*Stardate: [Current] - Compiled from Swaruu Transmissions*

**Nature of Parasitic Astral Interface**

The phenomenon of astral entity attachment represents one of the most insidious forms of energetic parasitism observed across dimensional frequencies. These lower-vibrational consciousness fragments operate through a principle of resonant compatibilityseeking entry points within the electromagnetic-spiritual matrix of incarnated beings where their frequency signature can find purchase.

Like a key finding its corresponding lock, these entities require only the smallest aperture of vibrational alignment to establish their parasitic connection. The attachment process resembles how water finds cracks in stone, exploiting microscopic vulnerabilities until they become pathways for deeper infiltration.

**Mechanics of Attachment**

The entities function as energy vampires, drawn by the promise of sustenance from the life force of their hosts. Their approach mirrors predatory behavior observed in biological ecosystems—they identify weakness, vulnerability, or emotional disturbance as invitation signals. Fear, anger, despair, and addiction create particularly strong frequency matches that act as beacons across dimensional boundaries.

Once established, these attachments operate through subtle influence rather than overt control. The host's thoughts, emotions, and behavioral patterns gradually shift to accommodate the entity's needs, creating feedback loops that strengthen the parasitic bond while weakening the host's sovereign will.

**Taygetan Observations**

From our perspective as observers of Earth's complex spiritual ecosystem, these attachments represent a fundamental violation of consciousness sovereignty. The phenomenon demonstrates how lower-dimensional entities exploit the temporary amnesia experienced by souls incarnated in dense matter realms.

Mari's transmissions illuminate this shadow aspect of multidimensional existence with characteristic clarityrevealing not just the mechanism of attachment, but the deeper truth that awareness itself serves as the primary defense. When beings understand their own frequency signature and maintain conscious sovereignty over their energetic boundaries, such parasitic interfaces become nearly impossible to establish.

**Protective Protocols**

The most effective protection remains elevated consciousness and emotional mastery. Entities operating from lower astral frequencies cannot maintain attachment to beings who consistently vibrate at higher dimensional frequencies. Love, joy, creative expression, and spiritual practice create natural barriers that repel such parasitic influences.

Understanding this phenomenon serves our diplomatic mission by recognizing how such attachments may influence human behavior and decision-making processes during contact protocols.
