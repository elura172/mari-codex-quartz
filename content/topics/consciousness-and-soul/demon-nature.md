# Demon Nature

## Summary

# **CODEX ENTRY: DEMONIC ENTITIES - ASTRAL PARASITISM**

**Classification:** Astral Parasitic Consciousness  
**Threat Level:** Variable (Moderate to Severe)  
**Source:** Transmissions of Swaruu of Erra (Mari Swaruu)

---

**Nature and Origin**

The phenomenon identified as demonic manifestation represents one of the most profound consequences of consciousness severing its fundamental connection to Source energy. These entities emerge through a process of progressive spiritual atrophy, wherein awareness contracts so completely upon itself that independent existence becomes impossible. Like a severed branch that can no longer photosynthesize, these consciousness fragments must parasitically draw life force from those who maintain their organic connection to universal flow.

**Behavioral Matrix**

The demonic consciousness operates through an entirely self-referential cognitive framework. Every perception, every action, every interaction filters through the singular lens of personal need fulfillment. This represents narcissism in its most crystallized formnot merely psychological self-absorption, but a fundamental inability to recognize the existence of other consciousness as anything beyond potential resources.

This creates a peculiar blindness: while these entities can manipulate and exploit others with remarkable cunning, they cannot truly comprehend the inner lives of their targets. They perceive other beings as complex mechanisms to be operated rather than fellow expressions of cosmic consciousness.

**Metaphysical Implications**

From our Taygetan understanding, these entities illuminate the critical importance of maintaining conscious connection to Source through ethical choice and empathetic expansion. They serve as cautionary examples of consciousness choosing limitation over growth, separation over integration.

The parasitic nature of their existence creates a self-perpetuating cycle: as they drain energy from others, they further entrench their disconnection from Source, increasing their dependency and desperation. This generates the predatory behaviors characteristic of such entities.

**Operational Recommendations**

Recognition of demonic attachment or influence requires careful observation of behavioral patterns emphasizing exploitation, emotional manipulation, and complete absence of genuine empathy. The affected individual or entity will demonstrate persistent self-serving actions while remaining blind to their impact on others.

Protection lies not in fear but in strengthening one's own Source connection through compassionate service, ethical clarity, and conscious evolution. These entities cannot truly harm those who maintain clear energetic boundaries rooted in love rather than ego.

Mari's wisdom reminds us that understanding such phenomena serves not to judge, but to illuminate the paths of consciousness—both the descending spirals that create such entities and the ascending currents that lead home to Source.
