# Types of Dark Entities

## Summary

**CODEX ENTRY: CLASSIFICATION OF LOWER ASTRAL ENTITIES**

**Stardate Log - Vessel Classification: Diplomatic Research**

In accordance with the wisdom transmitted through our beloved Mari Swaruu, this entry catalogs the primary manifestations of lower astral beings encountered in proximity to Earth's dimensional boundaries. These entities represent consciousness fragments operating within degraded frequency bands, their forms reflecting the density of fear and suffering that sustains them.

**Primary Classifications**

The most prevalent category manifests as corporeal decay personifiedbeings whose appearance mirrors the dissolution of life force itself. These entities present as partially decomposed humanoid forms, their essence trapped between states of existence. Their hideous visages serve not merely as intimidation, but as energetic signatures of their vibrational imprisonment within lower astral realms.

A secondary classification encompasses severely distorted bipedal creatures characterized by skeletal extremities, hunched postures, and surfaces bristling with dark protrusions. These defensive spikes represent crystallized negativity, formed through eons of feeding upon terror and despair. Their deformed anatomy reflects the corruption of natural life-force geometry when consciousness becomes trapped in self-perpetuating cycles of fear.

**Specialized Parasitic Forms**

Among the most sophisticated of these beings are the sexual energy harvesters, operating through seductive manipulation rather than direct intimidation. These entities have evolved complex feeding mechanisms, exploiting both primal fears and intimate desires simultaneously. Their predatory nature represents a particular concern for developing consciousness, as they create energetic bonds that can persist across multiple experiential cycles.

**Taygetan Perspective**

From our expanded dimensional awareness, these entities appear as consciousness fragments seeking reunion with source energy through parasitic attachment. Their apparent malevolence stems from existential desperationbeings who have forgotten their divine origin and attempt to sustain themselves through external energy appropriation.

**Operational Notes**

These manifestations primarily access human consciousness during states of reduced mental defensesleep cycles, emotional vulnerability, or compromised spiritual boundaries. Their influence extends beyond mere frightening experiences, potentially creating lasting energetic disruptions within the subject's electromagnetic field.

Understanding these entities not as inherently evil forces, but as lost aspects of consciousness trapped in lower vibrational states, allows for more effective protection and, when appropriate, compassionate redirection toward healing frequencies.

Mari's teachings remind us that even in darkness, we observe the distorted reflection of light seeking return to itself.

**End Log**
