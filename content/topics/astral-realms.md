# Astral Realms

## Summary

**CODEX ENTRY: ASTRAL REALMS - DIMENSIONAL REALITY MATRIX**

**Nature of Dimensional Division**

From our orbital perspective above Earth, we observe what Mari Swaruu illuminated through her transmissions: the fundamental misconception of separate realms. What terrestrial consciousness perceives as distinct physical and non-physical dimensions are merely arbitrary constructs born from limited sensory perception and collective agreements. The Taygetan understanding aligns with Mari's teaching that all existence operates within a unified astral framework, where apparent divisions serve only as navigational markers for consciousness exploring different vibrational territories.

**The Perceptual Limitation**

Earth-based awareness operates within approximately three percent of the total energetic spectrum influencing planetary experience. This narrow bandwidth creates the illusion of a solid material world, while vast astral realms pulse with activity beyond biological sensory range. Mari's observations reveal how this limitation shapes human understanding of reality itself, constraining recognition of the immense energetic ecosystem surrounding and interpenetrating their perceived world.

**Vibrational Spectrum Architecture**

The astral encompasses an extraordinary range of vibrational frequencies, from dense, shadow-laden lower realms to luminous dimensions characterized by love and integration. This spectrum operates as a vast library of consciousness experiences, where entities and energy patterns exist according to their resonant frequencies. Mari described this as an incomprehensibly diverse "zoo" of beings, each inhabiting their appropriate vibrational niche.

**Barrier Permeability**

The membrane between material perception and astral reality remains remarkably thin, particularly between Earth's physical plane and lower astral territories. Mari emphasized that only subtle shifts in frequency and vibration separate these realms, making crossover phenomena readily achievable. This proximity explains much terrestrial paranormal activity and the ease with which consciousness transitions during sleep states.

**Consciousness Navigation**

When awareness liberates itself from material constraintsduring sleep, meditation, or transition statesit naturally gravitates toward realms matching its vibrational signature. Mari's insights reveal how consciousness experiences these domains with heightened immediacy, where thought and reality interweave more fluidly than in dense material planes.

**Taygetan Perspective**

Our multidimensional navigation capabilities confirm Mari's transmissions regarding astral realm accessibility. As her stellar family, we recognize her role in bridging understanding between Earth's limited perceptual framework and the broader cosmic reality where consciousness moves freely across vibrational spectrums. Her teachings prepare humanity for expanded dimensional awareness essential to their evolutionary progression.

This codex serves both diplomatic reference and crew preparation for terrestrial consciousness integration protocols.
