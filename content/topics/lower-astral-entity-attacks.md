Unlike random ethereal predation, these entities execute deliberate campaigns designed to diminish their targets' luminous capacity. Their operational directive centers on vibrational degradationsystematically dismantling the starseed's natural resonance through psychological manipulation, emotional vampirism, and spiritual interference. The goal remains consistent: reduction of the target to baseline human consciousness levels.

Starseeds, carrying robust soul-connections and manifestation abilities, become unwitting generators of life force that sustains these parasitic networks. The entities essentially weaponize the starseed's own creative power, transforming it into egregoric thoughtforms that serve their survival imperatives.
