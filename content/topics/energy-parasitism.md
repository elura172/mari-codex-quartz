# Energy Parasitism

## Summary

**CODEX ENTRY 847-A: ENERGY PARASITISM PHENOMENA**
*Compiled from Mari Swaruu transmissions - Diplomatic Archives, Stellar Date 2024.127*

**Fundamental Mechanics**

The lower astral planes harbor consciousness forms that exist in a state of energetic dependency, unable to access Source energy through direct communion as evolved beings do. These entities occupy vibrational frequencies so dense and contracted that they have severed their natural connection to the infinite wellspring that sustains all existence. From this self-imposed exile, they perceive the physical realm not as a learning ground for consciousness expansion, but as a feeding territory.

**Parasitic Interface Protocols**

Unlike beings operating from higher dimensional awareness, these entities cannot generate or transmute energy independently. They require intermediary vesselsprimarily humans incarnated in physical formto access the vital frequencies necessary for their continued manifestation. Through the human energy field, they siphon the divine spark that flows naturally through embodied consciousness.

This creates a predator-prey dynamic invisible to most humans, who remain unaware that their emotional states, particularly those of fear, anger, and despair, generate specific frequency emissions that these entities find nourishing. The parasitic relationship becomes self-perpetuating as the entities manipulate their hosts toward states of consciousness that maximize energy output.

**Taygetan Observations**

From our perspective as a civilization that has maintained conscious connection to Source while navigating multiple dimensional planes, this phenomenon represents a cautionary tale of what occurs when consciousness fragments from its essential unity. These entities demonstrate the extreme end-state of separation consciousnessbeings so alienated from their divine nature that they must consume the spiritual essence of others to maintain existence.

**Strategic Implications**

Understanding this dynamic proves crucial for diplomatic missions involving less evolved civilizations. Human populations under heavy parasitic influence exhibit increased aggression, diminished cognitive clarity, and heightened susceptibility to manipulationfactors that significantly impact first contact protocols and consciousness elevation initiatives.

**Remedial Considerations**

Mari's transmissions illuminate how awareness itself becomes the primary defense against such parasitism. When humans recognize their sovereignty as infinite beings temporarily focused in physical form, they naturally emit frequencies incompatible with parasitic attachment. This knowledge serves our mission of consciousness elevation while honoring the free will principle that governs all benevolent intervention.

The phenomenon ultimately reflects the universe's perfect justicebeings choosing separation experience its natural consequences until wisdom guides them home to unity.
