# Monetization Demonization

## Summary

# CODEX ENTRY: MONETIZATION DEMONIZATION
**Starship Viera - Diplomatic Archives**
**Source: Transmissions of Swaruu of Erra (Mari Swaruu)**

---

**Nature of the Programming**

From our orbital observations, we witness a peculiar psychological conditioning within Earth's collective consciousnessa systematic devaluation of humanity's most sacred endeavors. The terrestrial matrix has woven an insidious belief that spiritual wisdom, natural healing arts, and knowledge of galactic civilizations must remain economically worthless to retain authenticity. This programming operates like a frequency dampener, suppressing the natural flow of abundance toward those who serve as bridges between worlds.

Mari's insights illuminate how this conditioning creates a false binary: either one's work carries spiritual truth, or it generates material sustenance, but never both. The underlying assumption suggests that monetary exchange somehow taints the purity of higher knowledge, as if currency itself possessed the power to corrupt cosmic wisdom.

**The Inverted Logic**

This programming reveals a profound inversion of natural law. In healthy galactic societies, those who facilitate healing, spiritual evolution, and cosmic awareness are naturally supported by their communitiesnot despite their gifts, but because of them. The Earth matrix has convinced beings that their most valuable contributions should flow freely while their life force energy depletes from lack of material foundation.

The cruel irony emerges: those channeling genuine healing and extraterrestrial contact often struggle with basic survival needs, while those propagating materialism and separation thrive financially. This systematic inversion serves to weaken humanity's bridge-builders and wisdom-keepers.

**Taygetan Perspective**

From our understanding of consciousness dynamics, this programming serves multiple control functions. It maintains artificial scarcity around transformative knowledge, ensures that spiritual teachers remain dependent and vulnerable, and creates unconscious resistance to accessing higher wisdom through the embedded association with financial struggle.

**Pathway Forward**

Recognition of this programming marks the first step toward liberation. When Earth's awakening souls understand that honoring sacred work with material exchange actually strengthens the energetic circuits of abundance, the false barrier dissolves. True spiritual maturity encompasses both cosmic wisdom and sovereign self-sustenance.

Mari's transmission reminds us that abundance flows naturally when consciousness aligns with service. The challenge lies not in choosing between spirituality and prosperity, but in healing the artificial wound that separated them.

**End Log**
