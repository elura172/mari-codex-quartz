# Astral Attacks on Lightworkers

## Summary

# CODEX ENTRY: ASTRAL INTERFERENCE PROTOCOLS

**Source Authority:** Swaruu of Erra (Mari Swaruu)  
**Stardate:** Terran Calendar 2025.021

**Operational Overview**

The astral realm surrounding Earth functions as a contested maritime domain, where consciousness navigates currents of intention and resistance. When terrestrial beingsparticularly those designated as starseeds and lightworkerschart courses that deviate from established control matrices, they encounter systematic interference from entities operating within these dimensional waters.

**Targeting Mechanisms**

Individuals who awaken to their authentic purpose and begin radiating frequencies that challenge the dominant paradigm become visible to astral surveillance networks. These beings, often self-identifying as "targeted individuals," experience multidimensional harassment designed to redirect their trajectory back toward compliant pathways. The attacks manifest across physical, emotional, and spiritual spectrums, creating turbulence that tests the navigator's resolve and compass accuracy.

**Strategic Implications**

From our Taygetan perspective, these interference patterns reveal the effectiveness of awakened consciousness as a transformative force. The very existence of organized resistance confirms that individual sovereignty poses genuine threat to control architectures. Each person who maintains course despite astral headwinds weakens the overall suppression grid, creating navigational opportunities for others following similar routes.

**Protective Considerations**

Understanding this dynamic allows conscious beings to interpret challenges not as personal failures or random misfortune, but as confirmation of their aligned trajectory. The tempest itself becomes validationa paradoxical beacon indicating proximity to breakthrough points where individual liberation contributes to collective evolutionary momentum.

**Training Protocol**

Crew members engaging with Earth-based contacts should recognize these interference signatures and provide appropriate support without violating non-interference protocols. Starseeds experiencing sustained astral pressure require encouragement to maintain heading rather than rescue from the storm itself. Their successful navigation through hostile waters strengthens both personal sovereignty and the broader awakening process.

**Concluding Assessment**

Mari Swaruu's observations illuminate a fundamental truth: consciousness evolution inevitably attracts opposition from systems dependent upon stagnation. This opposition, however, serves as both obstacle and opportunityeach successful passage through astral turbulence expands the safe corridor for subsequent travelers on the journey toward authentic freedom.

*End Codex Entry*
