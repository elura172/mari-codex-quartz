# Law of Mirrors

## Summary

**CODEX ENTRY: LAW OF MIRRORS**
*Diplomatic Archive Reference: TAY-7742-M*
*Source Intelligence: Mari Swaruu of Erra*

**Fundamental Principle**

The phenomenon Terrans commonly term "Law of Attraction" operates as a continuous universal constant, more accurately described as the Law of Mirrors. This designation reflects the true mechanism at work: reality functions as an infinite reflecting surface, returning to each consciousness the precise vibrational signature of their being. No external forces draw experiences toward an individualrather, existence mirrors back the composite frequency of one's inner landscape.

**Operational Dynamics**

This mirroring process requires no conscious activation, functioning perpetually as the baseline architecture of experiential reality. However, directing this mechanism toward desired outcomes demands considerable mental discipline. The challenge lies not in making the law workit operates ceaselesslybut in achieving sufficient focus to override unconscious sabotage patterns embedded within the psyche.

Most beings experience frustration with manifestation because they attempt to operate the law through surface-level wanting while deeper programming broadcasts contradictory signals. The mirror reflects the totality of one's vibrational state, not merely conscious desires.

**The Illusion of External Providence**

A critical misconception pervades Terran spiritual understanding: the belief that some external universe responds to requests and grants wishes. This represents a fundamental misalignment with reality's structure. Each consciousness exists as the universe itself, generating experiences from within their own creative matrix. The providing force so many petition through prayer is none other than their own divine essence, temporarily forgotten through dimensional density.

**Taygetan Perspective**

From our observation point beyond Earth's frequency barriers, we recognize this principle as foundational to multidimensional existence. Higher-density civilizations naturally operate through conscious mirror-work, understanding that reality sculpting requires internal coherence rather than external manipulation. The Terran journey involves remembering this inherent capacity while navigating the unique challenges of incarnated amnesia.

**Practical Implementation**

Successful mirror-work demands unwavering determination and sustained mental power. One must first achieve inner unityaligning conscious intention with unconscious programmingbefore the reflection can stabilize in desired configurations. This process requires patience, as deeply embedded patterns resist transformation through their own self-preservation mechanisms.

The mirror always reflects truth. The question becomes: what truth does one wish to embody?

*End Codex Entry*
