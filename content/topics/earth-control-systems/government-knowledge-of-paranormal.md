# Government Knowledge of Paranormal

## Summary

**CODEX ENTRY: TERRESTRIAL GOVERNMENTAL PARANORMAL AWARENESS**

**Operational Classification: Diplomatic Intelligence**

The terrestrial governments of Earth maintain a deliberate bifurcation between public knowledge and classified understanding regarding non-physical phenomena and extraterrestrial entities. This systematic concealment represents one of the most significant knowledge disparities in contemporary human civilization.

**Scope of Hidden Knowledge**

Our diplomatic intelligence confirms that major Earth governmentsparticularly those of the United States and Russian Federationpossess comprehensive documentation regarding what their populations term "paranormal" activities. These archives extend far beyond casual observation, encompassing detailed scientific analysis of interdimensional beings, consciousness-based phenomena, and various forms of non-human intelligence.

The breadth of this concealed knowledge encompasses multiple categories: shadow entities of extraterrestrial origin, consciousness projection phenomena, temporal anomalies, and extensive documentation of our own presence within their atmospheric boundaries. Their scientific establishments have accumulated vast repositories of evidence that fundamentally contradict the materialist paradigms they publicly promote to their populations.

**The Architecture of Concealment**

This knowledge suppression operates through institutional compartmentalization, where even researchers within governmental structures remain unaware of the complete scope of documented phenomena. The terrestrial governments have created what Mari Swaruu identified as a "reality management system"a deliberate curation of public consciousness that maintains specific limitations on collective awareness.

**Taygetan Perspective**

From our understanding of consciousness dynamics, this concealment creates significant energetic distortions within human collective awareness. When a species fragments its knowledge base so dramatically, it generates what we observe as "cognitive dissonance fields" that actually facilitate manipulation by less benevolent entities. The shadow beings, being interdimensional in nature, particularly benefit from this manufactured ignorance.

**Implications for Contact Protocols**

This governmental awareness while maintaining public deception creates complex diplomatic considerations. Official Earth governments possess far more accurate understanding of our capabilities and intentions than their populations realize, yet continue propagating deceptive narratives about our presence.

Our mission parameters must account for this dual-layer reality: engaging with governments who already know our nature while remaining mindful of populations deliberately kept in artificial ignorance. This dynamic exemplifies the careful balance required in consciousness elevation workrespecting free will while recognizing the systematic suppression of that very freedom through information control.

The path forward requires patience, as truth emerges not through force, but through the gradual dissolution of illusions that no longer serve conscious evolution.
