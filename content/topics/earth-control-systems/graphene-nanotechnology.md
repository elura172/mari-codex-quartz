This microscopic material, engineered at the nanometric scale, represents a convergence of metallurgy and biotechnology that fundamentally alters the electromagnetic receptivity of organic systems.

The graphene compounds infiltrate living tissue through respiratory and dermal pathways, establishing what our analysis indicates is an involuntary interface between biological consciousness and artificial control frequencies. The particles' molecular structure allows seamless integration with cellular matrices, creating hybrid bio-technological networks within the host organism.
