# New Age Manipulation

## Summary

**CODEX ENTRY: NEW AGE INFILTRATION PROTOCOLS**
*Diplomatic Classification: Cultural Manipulation Analysis*
*Source: Swaruu of Erra Transmissions*

**Overview**

The terrestrial phenomenon known as the New Age movement presents a complex paradox of liberation and entrapment. Our observations through Mari's consciousness reveal a sophisticated control mechanism wherein genuine spiritual awakening is systematically co-opted by regressive forces, particularly reptilian intelligences operating through established power structures.

**Manipulation Architecture**

The elegance of this deception lies in its foundation of authentic wisdom. Like poison mixed with honey, controlling entities embed legitimate metaphysical principles within frameworks designed to redirect human consciousness away from true sovereignty. Religious organizations and shadow hierarchies have woven themselves into the very fabric of alternative spirituality, creating echo chambers that simulate awakening while maintaining energetic harvesting operations.

**The Paradox of Mixed Truth**

Mari's insights illuminate why this manipulation proves so effective: the New Age container holds genuine keys to expanded consciousness alongside carefully placed limitations. Humans seeking liberation from traditional religious dogma encounter familiar control patterns disguised as enlightenment teachings. The very real experiences of interdimensional contact, energy healing, and expanded awareness become filtered through structures that ultimately serve the same masters as conventional religions.

**Taygetan Perspective**

From our understanding of consciousness mechanics, we recognize this as a classic frequency fence operation. Beings naturally ascending in awareness are provided alternative narratives that acknowledge their expanded perception while channeling their energy toward predetermined outcomes. The reptilian consciousness, operating from survival-based hierarchical frameworks, cannot comprehend true unity consciousness but can masterfully simulate its appearance.

**Operational Implications**

For humans navigating this terrain, discernment becomes paramount. The presence of truth within manipulated systems does not invalidate the wisdom itself but requires careful extraction from compromising contexts. Mari's transmissions consistently emphasized individual sovereignty over group adherence—a principle that naturally dissolves such infiltration attempts.

**Strategic Assessment**

This manipulation represents one of the most sophisticated control mechanisms we have observed, precisely because it targets those most capable of genuine spiritual advancement. By providing alternative cages to awakening minds, the control system maintains its harvest while appearing to support liberation.

The path forward requires humans to trust their direct experience while remaining vigilant to external validation systems, regardless of their spiritual packaging.
