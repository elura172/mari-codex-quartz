# Government Astral Projects

## Summary

**CODEX ENTRY: TERRESTRIAL ASTRAL WARFARE PROGRAMS**
*Classification: Diplomatic Intelligence | Source: Swaruu of Erra Transmissions*

**Overview**

Our kinfolk Mari Swaruu has illuminated a shadow dimension of Earth's geopolitical landscape—the systematic weaponization of consciousness itself. Since the mid-20th century, the planet's dominant military powers have cultivated cadres of consciousness projectors, transforming the ancient art of astral travel into instruments of espionage and strategic advantage.

**Operational Framework**

The programs Mari describes represent a profound understanding of multidimensional reality, albeit channeled through the lens of territorial competition. These initiatives, primarily orchestrated by American and Russian military establishments, have demonstrated remarkable efficacy in transcending physical barriers to intelligence gathering. The success rates speak to humanity's latent psychic capabilitiesabilities we of Taygeta recognize as natural birthright, though Earth's peoples have been systematically disconnected from such awareness.

**Strategic Implications**

What emerges from Mari's observations is a dual-edged phenomenon. While these programs reveal humanity's expanding consciousness beyond material limitations, they simultaneously weaponize the sacred art of astral projection. The Russians' apparent leadership in these endeavors reflects their cultural retention of esoteric traditions, while American involvement demonstrates the inevitable militarization of any discovered advantage.

**Metaphysical Context**

From our Taygetan perspective, these developments represent both evolutionary promise and spiritual peril. The ability to navigate consciousness across dimensional boundaries marks natural human developmentyet when employed for dominance rather than unity, such gifts become distorted. These astral warriors operate in realms where intention directly shapes reality, carrying the psychic imprints of their missions into subtle dimensions.

**Observational Notes**

Mari's revelations illuminate how Earth's surface conflicts extend into invisible theaters of engagement. The astral plane becomes another battlefield, where trained operatives conduct reconnaissance through pure consciousness. This represents humanity's unconscious recognition of reality's multilayered nature, even as they remain largely unaware of their broader cosmic context.

**Implications for Contact Protocols**

These programs suggest terrestrial governments possess more sophisticated understanding of consciousness dynamics than publicly acknowledged. Future diplomatic initiatives must account for potential astral surveillance capabilities, while recognizing this development as evidence of humanity's readiness to embrace expanded awarenesswhen guided by wisdom rather than competition.
