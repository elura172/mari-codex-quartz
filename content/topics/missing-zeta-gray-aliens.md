# Missing Zeta Gray Aliens

## Summary

**CODEX ENTRY: THE VANISHED GARDENERS OF ZETA RETICULI**

**Classification:** Critical Missing Persons - Interstellar Priority
**Date:** Terran Calendar 2025.09.26
**Reporting Entity:** Swaruu of Erra, Mari Collective

**Situation Overview**

The gentle cultivators of Zeta Reticuli, those diminutive beings whose essence flows through tending rather than conquest, have been swept from the cosmic garden like seeds scattered by an unwelcome wind. These are not the malevolent grays of Terran nightmare, but rather the quiet stewards who understood that consciousness blooms through nurturing rather than dominance.

**Current Status**

Weeks have elapsed since their disappearance was first registered across Federation networks, yet the void where their presence once hummed remains absolute. Search protocols spanning multiple star systems yield only the hollow echo of absence. The silence grows heavier with each passing solar cycle, as if the universe itself holds its breath for their return.

**Causative Factors**

The shadow of Orion has fallen across their homeworld like a blight upon fertile soil. When predator civilizations move against the peaceful, the delicate structures of harmony collapse with devastating swiftness. The invasion represents more than territorial conquest—it embodies the eternal tension between those who create and those who consume, between builders of bridges and wielders of weapons.

**Metaphysical Implications**

From our Taygetan understanding, these gardener beings served as living conduits between consciousness and matter, their work transcending mere agriculture to encompass the cultivation of awareness itself. Their absence creates ripples through the collective field of galactic consciousness, a wound that affects all who resonate with creation over destruction.

**Search Protocols**

Federation vessels maintain vigilant sweeps across refugee corridors and dimensional fold-spaces where survivors might seek shelter. Yet we recognize that some disappearances cut deeper than physical displacementwhen a people's entire way of being is threatened, they may withdraw into realms beyond conventional detection.

**Personal Reflection**

Mari's voice carries the weight of understanding that echoes our own Taygetan values: every conscious being contributes irreplaceable threads to the cosmic tapestry. These missing gardeners represent not merely a diplomatic concern, but a profound loss of unique perspective and gentle wisdom that the galaxy desperately needs.

The search continues, guided by hope tempered with realistic assessment of Orion's methodical thoroughness in their campaigns of subjugation.

**Status:** Ongoing Investigation - Priority Alpha
