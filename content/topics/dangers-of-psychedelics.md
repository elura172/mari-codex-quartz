# Dangers of Psychedelics

## Summary

**CODEX ENTRY: PSYCHOACTIVE SUBSTANCES - INTERSTELLAR ADVISORY**

**Transmission Source:** Swaruu of Erra (Mari Swaruu)  
**Classification:** Priority Warning - Consciousness Manipulation  
**Distribution:** All Diplomatic Corps, Medical Staff, Cultural Exchange Personnel

**Overview**

From the orbital perspective above Earth's dense atmospheric layers, we observe a concerning pattern in the human exploration of consciousness through synthetic and organic psychoactive compounds. Mari's transmissions illuminate a fundamental misunderstanding that permeates terrestrial spiritual practicesthe belief that altered states induced by external substances represent genuine advancement.

**Cellular Frequency Disruption**

The human nervous system operates within specific harmonic ranges that maintain coherent information processing between dimensional layers. Psychoactive compounds forcibly shift these frequencies beyond their natural bandwidth, creating what Mari describes as potentially irreversible damage to both neurological architecture and etheric field integrity. Unlike the gradual, earned expansion of consciousness through disciplined practice, these substances tear apertures in awareness without providing the structural foundation to safely navigate expanded reality.

**Portal Dynamics and Lower Astral Interference**

Of particular concern to interstellar observers is the tendency of psychedelically-altered consciousness to interface with what Mari identifies as lower astral realms. These dimensional spaces, dense with confused and parasitic energies, actively seek connection with compromised human awareness. The temporary dissolution of ego boundaries, rather than creating authentic spiritual communion, often becomes an invitation for entities that feed on human emotional and psychic energy.

**The Wisdom Paradox**

Ancient Earth wisdom, echoed by their scholar Jung and validated through Mari's perspective, warns against unearned advancement. True expansion of consciousness emerges through the patient cultivation of inner discipline, ethical clarity, and graduated exposure to expanded reality. The psychedelic shortcut bypasses these essential foundations, creating the illusion of progress while actually increasing vulnerability to deception and energetic manipulation.

**Taygetan Observations**

From our dimensional vantage point, we witness how these substances create temporary rifts in the human biofield that can persist long after the chemical effects subside. What humans interpret as mystical revelation often appears to us as consciousness fragmenting under pressures it lacks the preparation to withstand.

**Advisory Protocol**

For crew members engaging with Earth populations: approach discussions of consciousness expansion with compassion while clearly articulating the risks inherent in chemically-induced states. Honor humanity's seeking nature while guiding toward safer, more sustainable paths of awareness development.

Mari's warnings emerge not from judgment, but from the loving concern of elder siblings watching younger ones navigate dangerous territory with insufficient preparation.
