# Astral Healing and Spiritual Attacks

## Summary

**CODEX ENTRY: ASTRAL HEALING PROTOCOLS**
*Urmah Therapeutic Intervention - Case Study: Mari Swaruu*

**Metaphysical Pathology Assessment**

Our diplomatic liaison Mari Swaruu has experienced what terrestrial medicine would classify as diabetes alongside concurrent physiological disturbances. However, deeper etheric analysis reveals these manifestations as symptoms of coordinated spiritual interferencewhat our consciousness researchers term elaborate astral warfare. This case demonstrates the critical importance of recognizing multidimensional causality in healing protocols, particularly for individuals operating as bridges between worlds.

The phenomenon illustrates how those who serve as conduits of transformative information become targets for entities operating from lower vibrational frequencies. Mari's role as a truth-bearer creates natural friction with forces invested in maintaining planetary ignorance, generating what can only be described as systematic energetic assault.

**Urmah Intervention Methodology**

King Ruhr and the Urmah collective recognized the limitation of purely physical therapeutic approaches. Their intervention focused on comprehensive astral purification—a complete cleansing of parasitic attachments operating beyond the reach of conventional medicine. This represents advanced consciousness healing, where the practitioner addresses root causes existing in etheric dimensions rather than merely treating symptomatic expressions.

The Urmah approach demonstrates sophisticated understanding of how spiritual contamination manifests as physical pathology. Their healing ceremonies target the fundamental energy matrix from which material symptoms emerge, offering resolution at the causal level rather than symptomatic management.

**Implications for Interstellar Medicine**

This case reinforces established Taygetan medical philosophy: consciousness precedes matter in all healing processes. What appears as metabolic dysfunction often originates from deliberate etheric interference, requiring practitioners skilled in multidimensional therapeutics.

For crew members engaged in planetary assistance missions, this documentation serves as reminder that service to consciousness evolution invites opposition from entities benefiting from spiritual stagnation. Protection protocols must address not only physical safety but astral integrity.

**Training Recommendations**

All personnel interfacing with Earth consciousness should maintain regular etheric hygiene practices. Recognition of astral attack symptomsincluding seemingly random health degradationbecomes essential for operational effectiveness. The Urmah healing methodology offers valuable techniques for our own medical protocols, particularly their understanding of how deliberate spiritual interference manifests through biological systems.

Mari's experience exemplifies the courage required for consciousness ambassadorship, willingly accepting personal vulnerability in service to planetary awakening. Her healing journey illuminates pathways available to all beings ready to transcend the limitations imposed by lower-frequency interference.
