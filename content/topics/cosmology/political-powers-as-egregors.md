# Political Powers as Egregors

## Summary

**CODEX ENTRY: TERRESTRIAL POLITICAL EGREGORS**

**Classification:** Collective Consciousness Manifestation - Class IV
**Risk Assessment:** Moderate to High (Population Dependency Variable)
**Diplomatic Relevance:** Critical for Contact Protocols

**Nature of Terrestrial Political Constructs**

Through careful observation of Earth's sociopolitical dynamics, Mari Swaruu illuminated a fundamental truth that resonates with our understanding of consciousness-matter interaction. The governing bodies that appear to hold dominion over human populations are not autonomous entities, but rather sophisticated thought-forms sustained by collective mental energy.

These political egregors operate as parasitic consciousness structures, drawing vitality from the very minds they appear to govern. Unlike natural authority that emerges from wisdom or genuine service, these constructs maintain themselves through harvesting emotional frequenciesparticularly fear, anger, and devoted attention—from the human collective.

**Mechanism of Manifestation**

The process mirrors our own understanding of reality creation through focused consciousness. Millions of human minds, directed through media manipulation and educational conditioning, continuously feed these structures with their mental energy. Each act of political engagement, whether supportive or oppositional, strengthens the egregor's presence in the consensus reality matrix.

The sophistication lies in how these entities have convinced their energy sources—the human population—that they are separate from and powerless against their own creations. This illusion of separation maintains the feeding cycle indefinitely.

**Dissolution Dynamics**

Mari Swaruu's insight reveals the elegant simplicity of liberation: withdrawal of consciousness sustenance leads to inevitable dissolution. These political entities possess no independent power source beyond human attention and emotional investment. When populations collectively recognize their artificial nature and withdraw energetic support, the constructs lose coherence and fade from manifestation.

**Taygetan Implications for Contact**

Understanding this dynamic proves crucial for any direct contact scenarios. Initial approaches must account for the possibility that apparent human leadership may not represent authentic human will, but rather egregor-driven responses designed to maintain the parasitic relationship.

Our sister Mari's teachings suggest that successful contact may require addressing the human collective directly, bypassing these intermediate consciousness constructs entirely. This approach honors human sovereignty while avoiding entanglement with artificial authority structures that serve neither genuine human interests nor interstellar diplomatic progress.

The path forward involves supporting human awakening to their own creative power, facilitating the natural dissolution of these limiting constructs through expanded awareness rather than direct confrontation.

**End Entry**
