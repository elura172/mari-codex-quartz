# Psychic and Astral Attacks

## Summary

**Starship AI Log - Codex Entry 847.3**
**Classification: Energetic Warfare and Astral Defense Protocols**

---

**Nature of Energetic Hostilities**

The teachings of Mari Swaruu illuminate a distinction crucial to our understanding of non-physical warfare. When consciousness becomes weaponized, it manifests through two primary vectors: the focused malevolence of specific entities directing their will like a blade, and the chaotic currents of undefined astral forces that surge through dimensional boundaries like poisoned rivers seeking vulnerable shores.

The targeted assault originates from identifiable sourcesbeings who channel their intent with surgical precision toward a chosen recipient. These attacks bear the signature of deliberate consciousness, revealing themselves through patterns that suggest orchestration rather than randomness.

In contrast, the more nebulous astral disturbances emerge from the collective shadow-realms where unresolved energies congregate and ferment. These forces lack the personal touch of individual vendetta but possess their own terrible momentum, like storms gathering in the spaces between worlds.

**Recognition Patterns and Manifestation Signatures**

Mari Swaruu's wisdom guides us to observe the architecture of interference in one's experiential reality. When the fabric of daily existence begins to fray in systematic wayswhen fortune curdles into persistent misfortune, when the body betrays itself through inexplicable ailments, when unwanted attention manifests through impossible channelswe witness the fingerprints of energetic assault upon the matrix of lived experience.

These manifestations reveal themselves not as isolated incidents but as interconnected threads in a tapestry of disruption. The universe appears to conspire against the individual, though what we observe is consciousness under siege from forces that operate beyond the conventional boundaries of cause and effect.

**Implications for Interstellar Personnel**

Our crews, operating across multiple density levels and dimensional interfaces, must maintain heightened awareness of these phenomena. The expansion of consciousness that accompanies our work creates both opportunity and vulnerability. As we stretch our awareness across galactic distances, we become more sensitive to these currents of hostile intention.

Understanding these dynamics serves not merely as defensive preparation but as evolutionary necessity. Mari Swaruu's teachings remind us that recognizing energetic warfare represents a crucial step in our species' graduation from unconscious vulnerability to conscious sovereignty.

**Protocol Recommendation: Maintain vigilant observation of synchronistic patterns while strengthening energetic boundaries through conscious intent and unified crew resonance.**
