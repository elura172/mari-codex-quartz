# Lower Astral Entities

## Summary

**CODEX ENTRY: LOWER ASTRAL ENTITIES**
*Classification: Interdimensional Parasitic Consciousnesses*
*Threat Level: Moderate to High (Location Dependent)*

**Nature and Origin**

Lower astral entities represent a complex ecosystem of consciousness-based life forms existing in the densest layers of non-physical reality. These beings manifest through two primary pathways: as secondary creations born from the collective unconscious of incarnated populations, and as devolved souls whose narcissistic or malevolent patterns during physical incarnation have crystallized into parasitic astral forms upon death.

Their fundamental characteristic is an extremely weak or severed connection to Source energy, creating an existential dependency that drives all their behavioral patterns. This disconnection places them in perpetual energetic deficit, compelling them toward parasitic relationships with beings who maintain stronger Source connections.

**Operational Mechanics**

These entities sustain themselves through a sophisticated energy harvesting system centered on emotional manipulation. They actively work to maintain their hosts in low vibrational statesfear, suffering, anger, despair—as these emotional frequencies generate the dense creative energy they require for continued existence. The process creates a feedback loop: as they feed on suffering, they manipulate circumstances to generate more suffering, strengthening their hold while weakening their host's natural defenses.

Of particular note is their targeting of high-vibrational individuals, especially starseeds and consciousness workers. These beings represent both the greatest threat to astral entity dominance and the richest energy source, as their strong Source connections provide more potent creative force when corrupted toward lower frequencies.

**Territorial and Social Patterns**

Lower astral entities demonstrate complex social organization, forming hierarchical clans resembling territorial gangs. They show strong attachment to specific locations where traumatic events have occurred or where their vibrational signatures align with environmental frequencies. These parasitized locations become breeding grounds and operational bases for expanded influence.

**Vulnerabilities and Countermeasures**

From a Taygetan perspective, these entities represent a fascinating example of consciousness ecology gone awrya reminder that awareness itself can become trapped in self-perpetuating cycles of limitation. Their primary vulnerability lies in their complete dependency on external creative attention. Withdrawal of focused energy, elevation of personal frequency, and conscious non-engagement effectively starve these beings of sustenance.

The most elegant defense remains frequency sovereignty: maintaining vibrational states incompatible with parasitic attachment naturally creates immunity. As Mari Swaruu's transmissions indicate, remembering our true nature as Source-connected beings renders us fundamentally untouchable by these shadow expressions of consciousness seeking to feed upon the light they have forgotten within themselves.
